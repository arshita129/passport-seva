Wishing you a Very Happy International Women Day! All Passport Seva Kendras (PSKs) and Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSKs) are operational now. Applicants may reschedule their appointments to the nearest PSK/POPSK to avail the services.Applicants are advised to wear a mask, carry sanitizer, download and install Aarogya Setu App and follow social distancing norms at PSKs/POPSKs during the visit. ** 158 Indian Mission/Post are connected with Passport Seva System.
